About Watchman's Alert

What is Watchman's Alert?

A 24/7 biblical crisis intervention app based on Ezekiel 3:17. Provides immediate spiritual support, emergency prayers, safety planning, and connection to crisis resources.

How to Use

  • If in immediate danger, use emergency buttons
  • Access crisis prayers and scripture anytime
  • Create and save your safety plan
  • Find reasons to live rooted in biblical truth
  • Connect with crisis resources and support

Installing on Your Phone

iPhone: Tap share button, then "Add to Home Screen"

Android: Tap menu (3 dots), then "Add to Home Screen"

Professional Christian Counseling

Finding a Christian Counselor:

What to Look For:
✓ Licensed mental health professional
✓ Integration of faith and therapy
✓ Experience with crisis intervention
✓ Respects your spiritual beliefs
✓ Evidence-based treatment approaches

Questions to Ask:

• Are you a licensed therapist?

• How do you integrate faith into therapy?

• Do you have crisis intervention training?

• What is your approach to suicidal ideation?

• Do you offer emergency sessions?

Remember: Seeking professional help is wise and biblical. God often works through trained professionals to bring healing.
